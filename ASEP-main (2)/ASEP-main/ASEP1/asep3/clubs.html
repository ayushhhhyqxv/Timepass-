Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join a club? +
Click on the "Know More" button on any club card and follow the registration process.
Can I be part of multiple clubs? +
Yes, you can join multiple clubs based on your interests and time availability.
Are there any membership fees? +
Membership fees vary by club. Contact the club coordinator for specific details.
What is the time commitment? +
Time commitment varies by club, typically 2-4 hours per week including meetings and activities.
How do I start a new club? +
Submit a proposal to the student activities office with your club concept and required documentation.
